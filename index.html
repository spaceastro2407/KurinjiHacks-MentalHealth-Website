Welcome to this Website!

Being able to manage your mental health is a very important factor in everyday life. In fact it is just as important as you physical health! Teenagers these days face a legion of influences in school and home life. Not to mention the influences they face on social media, stress, and the internet. Such influences can impact a person' self esteem and affect them to a severe point. It can be difficult to manage your mental health, but you are not alone. Counselors, parents, and peers are willing to help you. This website is a tool that can help you connect with your (school) counselor when you need to talk to them. You just fill out a short form and it will record your response. Your counselor can check in and set up a time, where you can discuss about your mental health. Click the button to read the instructions!
